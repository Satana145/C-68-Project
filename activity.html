Student Council

Our Student Council is a student-run program with guidance from teacher advisors. Elections are usually held the first week of October and from then on, we have two meetings per month. One of the Student Council's goals is to fund educational and entertaining assemblies for the students. Furthermore, every month, the Student Council coordinates and completes both a fun activity and a service-related project.

Library

ALL students may check out 3 books every week. Books are checked out for a 1 week loan period. Ask your child what day their class visits the library, and remind them to return their books on time. Books are due the day before their library day. Renews may be kept until library day. Check outs have been postponed. Please help your child to remember when books are due, otherwise they will not be able to check out more books until the books are returned. Bills are processed for any books out for more than a month. Prices are set by the district.
